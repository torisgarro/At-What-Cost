Apple's Priority Quadrant

Instructions
  • Brainstorm a list of possible design features that can benefit your users’ long-term values.
  • Plot each feature according to effectiveness and technical/business feasibility.
  • Choose three of these features (or more) to incorporate into your final design.
Result

Apple’s priority quadrant allows its designers to brainstorm possible design solutions to the problems uncovered in previous exercises. By placing them according to effectiveness and feasibility, the team can see which features to go after. For example, Apple's quadrant reveals that designers should prioritize user feedback on device usage, control over notifications, and auto-reply.
