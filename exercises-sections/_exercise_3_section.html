Airbnb's Empathy Map

Instructions
  • Conduct interviews with people who represent the population indirectly affected by your solution.
  • Make an empathy map for each type of persona you discover in your interviews. Focus on what they say, think, do and feel.
Result

By creating this map, Airbnb’s designers gain empathy for those indirectly affected by their project. Understanding George’s needs and goals allows them to see the true reach of their product’s impact.
