Empathy Map

Examine the audience your solution will impact, directly and indirectly, and how.

Airbnb's Example

Experience designers already research their users’ needs and goals. It’s given that we will consider the people directly affected by our design – but what about those indirectly affected? In the case of Airbnb, designers created a product to give users new access to travel. However, this solution came at the expense of others' livelihood; low-income locals found themselves in an affordable housing crisis. Airbnb’s example shows that a design that serves one group can often exclude another.
