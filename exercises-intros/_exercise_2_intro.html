Backcasting

Consider what new problems the solution you’re designing might create.

Alexa's Example

Many have criticized the rise of feminized digital assistants as perpetuating negative gender stereotypes. Amazon’s Alexa, Apple’s Siri, and Microsoft’s Cortana all respond to a user’s request with a docile female voice, even when that request qualifies as sexual harassment. This premise seems obviously problematic now – so why didn’t Alexa's voice designers foresee the backlash? As these virtual assistants become more commonplace, it’s clear that this design solution solved one problem while exacerbating another.
